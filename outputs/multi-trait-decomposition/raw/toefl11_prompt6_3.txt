### Lexical Resource Description
Lexical Resource refers to the range and accuracy of vocabulary used in communication, assessing the ability to convey precise meanings and the control of lexical features.

### Score Intervals and Characteristics

- **Score 0-2**: Extremely limited vocabulary with little to no control over word formation and spelling; communication is severely impaired or impossible.

- **Score 3-4**: Very limited range of vocabulary; basic words are used repetitively or inappropriately, with frequent errors that strain comprehension.

- **Score 5-6**: Adequate vocabulary for the task, with some attempts at using less common words; occasional errors in spelling and word formation that do not significantly hinder communication.

- **Score 7-8**: Sufficient range of vocabulary that allows for flexibility and precision; skillful use of less common lexical items, though some inaccuracies may occur.

- **Score 9-10**: Wide range of vocabulary used fluently and flexibly, demonstrating sophisticated control; rare minor errors may occur but do not detract from overall effectiveness.
