### Grammatical Range and Accuracy

Grammatical Range and Accuracy assesses the variety and correctness of sentence structures used in writing or speaking.

### Score Intervals

- **Score 0-2**: 
  - Limited or no ability to use sentence forms; communication is severely impaired, relying on memorized phrases or showing no grammatical understanding.

- **Score 3-4**: 
  - Attempts to use sentence forms but frequent grammatical and punctuation errors distort meaning; very limited range of structures with rare use of complex sentences.

- **Score 5-6**: 
  - Mix of simple and complex sentences with some grammatical errors; attempts at complexity may lead to inaccuracies, but communication is generally clear.

- **Score 7-8**: 
  - Good control of a variety of complex structures; most sentences are error-free with only occasional minor errors that do not impede understanding.

- **Score 9-10**: 
  - Exceptional use of a wide range of structures with full flexibility and accuracy; errors are rare and typically minor, demonstrating a high level of grammatical proficiency.
