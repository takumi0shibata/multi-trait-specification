### Lexical Resource Description
Lexical Resource refers to the range and accuracy of vocabulary used in communication, assessing the ability to convey precise meanings and the control of lexical features.

### Score Intervals and Characteristics

- **Score 0-2**: Extremely limited vocabulary with little to no control over word formation and spelling; communication is severely impaired or impossible.

- **Score 3-4**: Very limited range of vocabulary with basic words; frequent errors in word formation and spelling may cause strain for the reader, making comprehension difficult.

- **Score 5-6**: Adequate vocabulary for the task, with some attempts at using less common words; occasional inaccuracies and errors in spelling or word formation that do not significantly hinder communication.

- **Score 7-8**: Sufficient to wide range of vocabulary used flexibly and with some sophistication; includes less common lexical items with occasional inaccuracies, but overall conveys precise meanings effectively.

- **Score 9-10**: Extensive and sophisticated vocabulary with natural control; conveys nuanced meanings with rare minor errors, demonstrating a high level of lexical proficiency.
