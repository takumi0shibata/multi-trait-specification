### Lexical Resource Description
Lexical Resource refers to the range and accuracy of vocabulary used in communication, assessing the ability to convey precise meanings and the control of lexical features.

### Score Range Intervals

- **Score 0-2: Extremely Limited Vocabulary**
  - Characterized by an extremely restricted range of vocabulary with little to no control over word formation and spelling, resulting in severe communication difficulties.

- **Score 3-4: Very Basic Vocabulary**
  - Utilizes only a very limited range of words and expressions, with basic vocabulary that may be repetitive or inappropriate, leading to noticeable errors that strain comprehension.

- **Score 5-6: Adequate Vocabulary**
  - Demonstrates an adequate range of vocabulary for the task, with attempts to use less common words, though inaccuracies may occur; errors in spelling and word formation are present but do not significantly impede communication.

- **Score 7-8: Sufficient to Wide Range of Vocabulary**
  - Employs a sufficient to wide range of vocabulary with some flexibility and precision, including less common lexical items; occasional inaccuracies in word choice and collocation may occur, along with rare spelling errors.

- **Score 9-10: Sophisticated Vocabulary Use**
  - Exhibits a wide range of vocabulary with natural and sophisticated control, conveying precise meanings effectively; rare minor errors may occur but do not detract from overall communication quality.
