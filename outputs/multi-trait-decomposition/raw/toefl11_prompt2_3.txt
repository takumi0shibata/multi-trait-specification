### Lexical Resource Description
Lexical Resource refers to the range and accuracy of vocabulary used in communication, assessing the ability to convey precise meanings and the control of lexical features.

### Score Intervals and Characteristics

- **Score 0-2**: Extremely limited vocabulary with little to no control over word formation and spelling, severely hindering communication and understanding.

- **Score 3-4**: Very basic vocabulary used repetitively or inappropriately, with significant errors in word formation and spelling that may cause strain for the reader.

- **Score 5-6**: Limited but adequate vocabulary for the task, with some attempts at using less common words; errors in spelling and word formation are present but do not impede overall communication.

- **Score 7-8**: Sufficient to wide range of vocabulary used flexibly and fluently, with occasional inaccuracies in word choice or collocation; rare errors in spelling or word formation may occur.

- **Score 9-10**: Extensive and sophisticated vocabulary used with natural control, conveying precise meanings with very few minor errors, demonstrating a high level of lexical proficiency.
