### Lexical Resource Description
Lexical Resource refers to the range and accuracy of vocabulary used in communication, assessing the ability to convey precise meanings and the control of lexical features.

### Score Intervals and Characteristics

- **Score 0-2**: Extremely limited vocabulary with little to no control over word formation and spelling; communication is severely impaired or impossible.

- **Score 3-4**: Very limited range of vocabulary with basic words used repetitively; frequent errors in word formation and spelling that may cause strain for the reader.

- **Score 5-6**: Limited to adequate vocabulary that meets basic task requirements; some attempts at using less common words, but with noticeable inaccuracies and occasional errors that do not impede overall communication.

- **Score 7-8**: Sufficient to wide range of vocabulary used flexibly and fluently; includes less common lexical items with some awareness of style and collocation, though occasional inaccuracies may occur.

- **Score 9-10**: Extensive and sophisticated vocabulary with natural control; conveys precise meanings with rare minor errors, demonstrating a high level of lexical proficiency.
