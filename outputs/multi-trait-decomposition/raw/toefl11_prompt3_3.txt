### Lexical Resource Description
Lexical Resource refers to the range and accuracy of vocabulary used in communication, assessing the ability to convey precise meanings and the control of lexical features.

### Score Range Intervals

**Score: 0-2**
- **Characteristics:** Extremely limited vocabulary with no control over word formation or spelling; communication is nearly impossible.

**Score: 3-4**
- **Characteristics:** Very limited range of vocabulary with basic expressions; frequent errors in word formation and spelling that may distort the message.

**Score: 5-6**
- **Characteristics:** Adequate vocabulary for the task with some attempts at less common words; occasional inaccuracies and errors that do not significantly impede communication.

**Score: 7-8**
- **Characteristics:** Sufficient to wide range of vocabulary used flexibly and fluently; skillful use of uncommon lexical items with minor inaccuracies in word choice or spelling.

**Score: 9-10**
- **Characteristics:** Extensive and sophisticated vocabulary with natural control; rare minor errors occur only as slips, allowing for precise and nuanced expression.
