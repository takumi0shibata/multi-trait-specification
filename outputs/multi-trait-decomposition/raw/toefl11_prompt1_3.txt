### Lexical Resource Description
Lexical Resource refers to the range and accuracy of vocabulary used in communication, assessing the ability to convey precise meanings and the control of lexical features.

### Score Intervals and Characteristics

- **Score 0-2**: Extremely limited vocabulary with little to no control over word formation and spelling, severely hindering communication and understanding.

- **Score 3-4**: Very basic vocabulary used repetitively or inappropriately, with noticeable errors in word formation and spelling that may cause strain for the reader.

- **Score 5-6**: Limited but adequate vocabulary that meets the task requirements; some attempts at using less common words may lead to inaccuracies, but communication remains mostly clear.

- **Score 7-8**: A sufficient range of vocabulary that allows for flexibility and precision; includes less common lexical items with occasional inaccuracies, but overall demonstrates good control and fluency.

- **Score 9-10**: A wide and sophisticated range of vocabulary used fluently and flexibly, with very few minor errors, showcasing exceptional control over lexical features and precise meanings.
