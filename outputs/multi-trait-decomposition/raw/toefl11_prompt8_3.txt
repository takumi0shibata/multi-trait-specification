### Lexical Resource Description
Lexical Resource refers to the range and accuracy of vocabulary used in communication, assessing the ability to convey precise meanings and the control of lexical features.

### Score Intervals and Characteristics

- **Score 0-2**: Extremely limited vocabulary with no control over word formation or spelling; communication is severely impaired or impossible.

- **Score 3-4**: Very limited range of vocabulary with basic words; frequent errors in word formation and spelling that may cause strain for the reader.

- **Score 5-6**: Adequate vocabulary for the task, with some attempts at less common words; occasional inaccuracies and errors that do not significantly impede understanding.

- **Score 7-8**: Sufficient range of vocabulary that allows for flexibility and precision; skillful use of less common lexical items, though some inaccuracies may occur.

- **Score 9-10**: Extensive and sophisticated vocabulary with natural control; rare minor errors that do not detract from the overall effectiveness of communication.
