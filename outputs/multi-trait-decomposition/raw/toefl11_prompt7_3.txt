### Lexical Resource Description
Lexical Resource refers to the range and accuracy of vocabulary used in communication, assessing the ability to convey precise meanings and the control over lexical features.

### Score Range Intervals

**Score: 0-2**
- **Characteristics:** Extremely limited vocabulary with no control over word formation or spelling; communication is nearly impossible.

**Score: 3-4**
- **Characteristics:** Very limited range of vocabulary with basic words; frequent errors in word formation and spelling that may hinder understanding.

**Score: 5-6**
- **Characteristics:** Adequate vocabulary for the task; attempts to use less common words but with some inaccuracies; occasional spelling errors that do not impede communication.

**Score: 7-8**
- **Characteristics:** Sufficient to wide range of vocabulary used flexibly and fluently; some uncommon lexical items are used accurately, though occasional inaccuracies may occur.

**Score: 9-10**
- **Characteristics:** Extensive and sophisticated vocabulary with natural control; rare minor errors occur only as slips, demonstrating a high level of lexical proficiency.
