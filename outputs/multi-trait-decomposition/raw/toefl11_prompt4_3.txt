### Lexical Resource Description
Lexical Resource refers to the range and accuracy of vocabulary used in communication, assessing the ability to convey precise meanings and the control over word formation and spelling.

### Score Intervals and Characteristics

- **Score 0-2**: Extremely limited vocabulary; minimal control over word formation and spelling, severely hindering communication.
  
- **Score 3-4**: Very basic vocabulary with frequent errors; limited ability to convey meaning, causing strain for the reader.

- **Score 5-6**: Adequate vocabulary for the task; some attempts at using less common words, but with noticeable inaccuracies that do not significantly impede understanding.

- **Score 7-8**: Sufficient range of vocabulary with some flexibility and precision; occasional inaccuracies in word choice or collocation, but generally effective communication.

- **Score 9-10**: Wide range of sophisticated vocabulary used fluently and flexibly; rare minor errors occur, demonstrating a high level of lexical control and precision.
